ABOUT THE SCHOOL
Lwandai secondary school started way back before the end of colonialism, It started as a missionary school in 1950 with 58 students under the hands of DR, Daniel Frielberg and pastor Herbert Uhlin ,and the next class had 25 student.Since then the school continued to exist, performing better in Art subjects mostly and raising children in a God fearing way.
Lwandai offers O-level and A-level studies involving CBG,PCB,PCM,PGM,HKL,HGK,HGL,EGM and CBN in O-level all basic subjects are offered plus Agriculture.
Bible knowledge and Divinity are compulsory subjects to both levels of education
The school staff is adequate for the good performance of students
