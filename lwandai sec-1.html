Lwandai secondary is a church owned school located in Tanga Tanzania, it is one of the oldest schools in Tanzania with many graduates since its start.

The school is specifically owned by the NORTH EASTERN DIOCESE OF THE EVENGELICAL LUTHERAN CHURCH IN TANZANIA, And it base on raising children in knowing GOD without forgeting giving the best and quality worldly education for the better future
